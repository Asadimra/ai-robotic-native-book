# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

This textbook serves as an introduction to the fascinating and rapidly evolving field of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, designed for undergraduate students. Here, you will explore the convergence of artificial intelligence with embodied systems, learning how intelligent agents perceive, reason, and act within the physical world.

We will cover fundamental concepts, historical developments, and cutting-edge applications, providing a solid foundation for understanding the robots of today and designing the intelligent systems of tomorrow.
